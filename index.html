    About us

    The Royal Cafe is an enviromentally friendly PAYF (pay as you feel) cafe. We are a non-profit organisation which was founded by Meghan and Harry to help combat food waste created by major supermarkets and distributors.

    What We Do

    We use 'diverted' food in all of our meals and products. Before you start panicking hear us out! Did you know 1.3 billion tonnes of food is wasted every year? Diverted food is food which is perfectly useable and edible but is rejected by supermarkets and distributors because it doesn't 'look' good on their shelves. So instead of wasting it we use it to create delicious meals!

    Our Targets

    Our main goals are to make it easier for people who are struggling financially to still be able to access fresh food and then can contribute by their skills and timne in lieu of payment. We also accept volunteer time on the floor, kitchen or in 'The Box' (our fresh fruit and vegetable warehouse).
